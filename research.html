Research

My Research

My research involves studying the impact of AGN feedback and fueling on galaxy evolution by using the Feedback In Realistic Environments (FIRE) simulations. My dissertation focuses on the impact of AGN feedback on the evolution of galaxies. I am also working on a project that studies the impact of gas inflows on the evolution of galaxies.
